My Approach

How we'll work together

In our work together, you'll connect with different parts of yourself with curiosity and compassion. You'll practice returning to the here and now when old patterns or anxieties pull you away.

Over time, you'll build a kinder relationship with yourself and develop real skills you can use long after therapy ends—leaving you feeling grounded, empowered, and ready to move forward.

I take a warm, humanistic approach, drawing on Internal Family Systems (IFS) for trauma work and mindfulness to help you stay present and grounded.

The goal is empowerment, not dependence. You'll leave with the skills you need to navigate life's challenges on your own.

Investment

Private pay, by design

Sessions are $200, and I don't work with insurance—no claims, no superbills, no diagnostic codes required.

This is intentional. When insurance isn't part of the equation, our work stays focused entirely on you—not on checking boxes, justifying treatment, or fitting your experience into a billing category. You get to define what we work on and how long we work together.

It also means complete privacy. Nothing we discuss becomes part of an insurance record.
